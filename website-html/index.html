About Triton Groups

Connect with students from the same class, club, or community

Our goal

Distance learning has become the new norm in education since the advent of school closures as a response to the Covid-19 pandemic. It has always been a challenge to connect with other students from the same class or community and distance learning only complicates the process. Our goal is to serve as a platform where students can connect with their classmates through the use of Discord servers. Discord is a powerful messaging platform that allows users to make extremely oganized and useful group chats. It will allow students to develop connections with their peers, create study groups, or simply serve as an outlet for students to come together to share experiences and learn from one another. Our web platform will help you find the discord server for your class or community. If a discord server does not exist for your class, we make it easy for you to create one with our ready-available template and bots.

Our web platform provides more than just discord servers. USCD hosts a variety of clubs and organizations. Our web platform will make it easy for students to locate their club of interest and connect with other students that share the same passion. Our club suggestions will also help students find the right club or organization that fits their needs.

Social distance should not equate to social disconnect. Triton Groups was created in hopes of helping Tritons make the same connections that they would in person.
